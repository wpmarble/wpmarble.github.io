Ballot Measures Database

2016 ballot measures are being added as they are qualified by each state to be on the ballot this year.  The last measures will be verified by the end of the summer.

NCSL’s Ballot Measures Database includes all statewide ballot measures, starting over a century ago.

The database is searchable by topic area, with options such as abortion, bond measures, criminal justice, education, elections, health, labor and employment, natural resources, tax and revenues, transportation and more. The database is updated as new ballot measures qualify to be on the ballot; it now includes all ballot measures that have qualified for 2015. On election night, each entry is updated with “pass” or “fail.”

This database is provided for informational purposes only. For details on ballot measures, please contact the appropriate state’s election officials. 

For more information about the database, contact NCSL’s elections team via email or at 303-364-7700.
